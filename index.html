About Me

My name is Kenneth, but you can call me K.C. I've lived in Newport News, VA for all 25 years of my life; but I hope to live a more rural life, starting in the near future. If I'm not at work maintaining handtool and vending stock for Huntington Ingles, then I'm either cooking at home, spending money at Lidl or participating at church.

I'm currently a student in the coding bootcamp at the University of Richmond and will graduate in May 2021. As recent as 4 months ago, I would've never thought I'd make a career of coding. It makes sense though because I love building things, although the things I'm used to building are more physical (like wood furniture) than digital (like a website). This will simply utilize my passion for building things in a new format.

This course is a great way to start but I plan to go beyond it's scope and gain skills in programming cross-platform apps. My ultimate goal is to be a freelancer, where I can offer my services to help anyone I can, whether it's a local start-up in need of a website or someone who has a great idea for an app but no avenue in which to build it. I've been skydiving so when they say the sky is the limit, I take it literally.
